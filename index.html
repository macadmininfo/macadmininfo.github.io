Welcome, Mac and Apple Admins

So, you're new to DevOps/System Administration. Or, your're a long-time admin, but have just started being responsible for Macs/iOS. Or, you've been in the Mac space since 1984, and are just looking for some new resources. You're in the right place!
The focus of this list is getting started from square one, and then improving/growing once you're in the role. It also intends to help with issues unique to managing Macs and iOS devices. (So, while a Mac Admin may want to set up Docker on AWS to build out Mac-management infrastructure, that's outside of the scope. Learning bash and the terminal are important for a Mac admin, but neither of those are really unique to the Mac. There is a fine line at times...it's a judgement call.)
Finally, your contributions are welcome! This site is hosted on GitHub so please send pull requests.
